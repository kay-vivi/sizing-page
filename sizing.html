Size Guide

Almost every item we sell is designed with four-way stretch fabric. This means there's quite a bit of latitude for selecting your size. If you like a fitted look or more compression, you may be able to size down. If you like a looser fit, select your normal size, or size up.

Measuring Tips

Bust

Measure just below the bust where the bra band sits. Measuring tape should be parallel to the floor.

Waist

Measure at the belly button (also considered the natural waistline).

Hips

Measure at the fullest part of the hips while standing with your feet together.

Inseam

Measure on the inside of your leg from the pubic bone to your ankle
